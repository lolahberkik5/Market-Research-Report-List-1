<p><p>The Disposable Vinyl Gloves market is segmented into two main types: Powered Vinyl Gloves and Powder-Free Vinyl Gloves. Powered Vinyl Gloves are coated with a light dusting of powdered cornstarch, facilitating easy donning and doffing. They are often preferred in situations where quick changes are necessary. In contrast, Powder-Free Vinyl Gloves are manufactured without powder, reducing the risk of allergic reactions and contamination, making them suitable for sensitive environments such as food preparation and healthcare settings.</p></p>
