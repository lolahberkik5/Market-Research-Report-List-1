<p><p>Var Compensators are essential for maintaining voltage stability in power systems. The market types include TCR-typed SVC, which utilizes Thyristor Controlled Reactors to regulate reactive power; MCR-typed SVC, employing Magnetic Controlled Reactors for dynamic response; and TSC-typed SVC, which integrates Thyristor Switched Capacitors for improved efficiency. Each type offers unique benefits for reactive power compensation, contributing to grid reliability and operational flexibility, making them crucial for managing voltage levels in electrical networks.</p></p>
