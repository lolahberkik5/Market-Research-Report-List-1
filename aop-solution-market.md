<p><p>Advanced Oxidation Processes (AOP) markets encompass various techniques for wastewater treatment, each utilizing different oxidation methods. Photochemical AOP employs light to generate reactive radicals, while Catalytic Wet AOP uses catalysts to enhance oxidation reactions in aqueous solutions. Sonochemical AOP relies on ultrasound to produce radicals, and Ozone AOP involves the application of ozone for detoxification. Electrochemical AOP utilizes electrical energy to initiate oxidation, whereas Fenton AOP combines hydrogen peroxide with ferrous iron to generate hydroxyl radicals, effectively degrading pollutants. Together, these methods offer versatile solutions for various environmental challenges.</p></p>
