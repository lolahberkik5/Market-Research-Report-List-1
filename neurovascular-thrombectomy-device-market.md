<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Neurovascular Thrombectomy Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical/Fragmentation Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Stent Retrievers</li><li>Basket/Brush Retrievers</li><li>Coil Retrievers</li><li>Aspiration Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Rheolytic/Hydrodynamic Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Ultrasonic Thrombectomy Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurovascular thrombectomy device market comprises various types aimed at removing thrombi from cerebral vessels. Mechanical/Fragmentation devices are designed to physically break up clots. Stent retrievers capture and extract clots via expandable stents. Basket/brush retrievers employ baskets or brushes to ensnare clots. Coil retrievers use coils to trap and remove thrombi. Aspiration devices suction out clots. Rheolytic/hydrodynamic devices break down clots through high-speed fluid jets, while ultrasonic devices utilize sound waves for clot fragmentation and removal.</p></p>
