<p><strong>The Medium and Heavy-duty Truck Steering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electric Power Steering</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium and heavy-duty truck steering system market comprises three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), which uses hydraulic fluid to assist steering, providing robust control and feedback; Electric Power Steering (EPS), an energy-efficient system that utilizes electric motors for enhanced responsiveness and reduced weight; and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), which combines hydraulic assist with electric control for improved efficiency and adaptability. Each system offers unique benefits, catering to varying performance, efficiency, and operational needs in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
