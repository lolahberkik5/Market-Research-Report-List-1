<p><p>The progressive cavity pump market is categorized into three main types: single screw, double screw, and three screw pumps. Single screw pumps utilize a single helical screw to move fluids, offering high efficiency for viscous substances. Double screw pumps use two intermeshing screws for better flow and pressure management, suitable for challenging applications. Three screw pumps incorporate three screws, providing smooth and continuous flow, ideal for high-pressure requirements. Each type serves specific industrial needs, enhancing fluid transfer capabilities.</p></p>
