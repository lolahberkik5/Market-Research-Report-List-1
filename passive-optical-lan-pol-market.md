It typically employs passive components like splitters, reducing maintenance costs and energy consumption. The market features two main types: GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network), known for its high-speed capabilities, and EPON (Ethernet Passive Optical Network), which integrates Ethernet technologies for simpler deployment. Both types support various applications, offering scalable solutions for modern communication needs.</p></p>
