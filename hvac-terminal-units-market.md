<p><p>HVAC terminal units are essential for controlling airflow and temperature in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. Single duct units deliver conditioned air through one duct, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Dual duct units utilize two ducts—one for hot air and one for cold—allowing precise temperature control in different zones. Fan-powered terminal units enhance air mixing and distribution, featuring a small fan to boost airflow and improve comfort levels. Each type caters to specific climate control needs in commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
