<p><p>Evaporative crystallizers are specialized industrial equipment designed to concentrate solutions and facilitate the crystallization of dissolved solids. They function by evaporating a portion of the solvent, typically water, from a solution, leading to the supersaturation of solutes which subsequently crystallize. These systems are essential in various industries, including chemicals, pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and environmental management, particularly for waste treatment.</p><p>The Evaporative Crystallizers Market is anticipated to witness significant growth, with expectations of a 15% CAGR during the forecast period.
