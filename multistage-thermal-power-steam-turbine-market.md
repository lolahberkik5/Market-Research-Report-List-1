In contrast, back pressure turbines utilize exhaust steam for heating or industrial processes, offering utility in specific applications where energy recovery is essential. Other types may include controlled extraction turbines or regenerative steam turbines, each catering to unique operational needs in power generation and thermal management systems, enhancing overall efficiency.</p></p>
