Ethylene chlorotrifluoroethylene (ECTFE) and ethylene tetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) serve in corrosive environments. Fluorinated ethylene propylene (FEP) and perfluoroalkoxy (PFA) are valued for their thermal stability and flexibility. Together, these materials cater to various industrial applications, enhancing product performance.</p></p>
