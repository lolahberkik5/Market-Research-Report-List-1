<p><p>The Digital Audio and Video Decoder market is segmented primarily into two types based on output ports: Optical Output Port Digital A/V Decoders and Coaxial Output Port Digital A/V Decoders. Optical output port decoders use fiber-optic cables to transmit high-quality audio and video signals, offering superior sound clarity, especially for home theater systems. In contrast, coaxial output port decoders utilize coaxial cables for digital audio transmission, providing a reliable connection for various audio devices, often at a lower cost. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs in home entertainment systems.</p></p>
