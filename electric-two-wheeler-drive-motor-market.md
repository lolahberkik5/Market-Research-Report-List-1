<p><ul><li>Central Motor</li><li>Hub Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric two-wheeler drive motor market mainly comprises central motors and hub motors. Central motors, mounted near the bike’s center, provide balanced weight distribution and better handling, making them ideal for performance bikes. Conversely, hub motors are integrated into the wheel hubs, offering simplicity and low maintenance, which is suitable for commuter models. Each type caters to different consumer needs, with central motors excelling in efficiency and power, while hub motors focus on ease of use and compact design.</p></p>
