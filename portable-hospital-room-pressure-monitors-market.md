<p><p>The Portable Hospital Room Pressure Monitors market features two main types: Negative Sensor Monitoring and Differential Sensor Monitoring. Negative Sensor Monitoring focuses on maintaining a negative pressure environment, crucial for isolation rooms to prevent airborne contaminants from escaping. In contrast, Differential Sensor Monitoring measures the pressure difference between two spaces, ensuring that sterile areas remain uncontaminated. Both types enhance infection control and patient safety, making them essential tools in modern healthcare facilities for effective environmental management.</p></p>
