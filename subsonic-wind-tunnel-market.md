<p><strong>The Subsonic Wind Tunnel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Wind Tunnel</li><li>Closed Wind Tunnel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsonic wind tunnel market is divided into two main types: open wind tunnels and closed wind tunnels. Open wind tunnels allow air to flow freely around the test object and typically feature a simpler design, making them cost-effective for various applications. In contrast, closed wind tunnels enclose the test section within a circular or rectangular duct, providing better control over airflow and environmental conditions, facilitating more precise measurements. Each type serves specific research purposes and exhibits distinct advantages.</p></p>
