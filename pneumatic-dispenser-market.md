<p><p>The pneumatic dispenser market is segmented into two primary types: single-component and two-component dispensers. Single-component dispensers are designed to handle one type of material, offering simplicity and ease of use for applications requiring a single adhesive or sealant. In contrast, two-component dispensers are engineered to mix and dispense two different materials simultaneously, providing enhanced bonding capabilities and versatility for applications like epoxy resins and advanced coatings. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing efficiency and precision in material handling.</p></p>
