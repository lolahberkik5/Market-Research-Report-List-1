<p><p>The hybrid video surveillance market comprises two main types: analog and IP video surveillance systems. Analog video surveillance systems utilize traditional coaxial cable connections for transmitting video signals, offering a cost-effective solution for smaller installations. In contrast, IP video surveillance systems leverage network connections, providing higher resolution, advanced analytics, and remote viewing capabilities. The hybrid approach combines both technologies, allowing organizations to integrate existing analog infrastructure with new IP capabilities, enhancing flexibility and scalability in surveillance setups.</p></p>
